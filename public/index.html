Relay

The webhook & event-relay platform for B2B SaaS.

Relay handles the unsexy half of every modern SaaS — outbound webhooks, inbound event ingestion, retries, signing, replay, observability, and per-customer rate limits. One reliable layer between your services and your customers' integrations.

Reliable delivery

Sub-second fan-out at 40M+ events / day with exactly-once semantics and full replay.

Secure by default

HMAC signing, automatic key rotation, and per-tenant signing keys out of the box.

Drop-in SDKs

Idiomatic clients for Node, Python, Go, and Ruby. Five lines from install to first event.

Audit & observability

Full delivery log, OpenTelemetry traces, and Prometheus metrics — wired in, not bolted on.

Process

How an engagement runs.

We use the same playbook on every project, refined over a decade of shipping software. It's deliberately boring — because boring ships, and ships on time.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We meet your team, read the codebase, and write a sharp project brief — scope, risks, milestones, and a fixed-fee proposal.

    ~ 1 week · fixed fee
  2. 02

    Architecture

    A clear technical plan: data model, service boundaries, integrations, and the smallest credible v1. Reviewed and signed off before code.

    ~ 1–2 weeks
  3. 03

    Build

    Weekly demos, fortnightly retros, transparent invoicing. You see progress every Friday and ship to production every sprint.

    2–6 months
  4. 04

    Operate

    Optional ongoing support: on-call, performance tuning, scale-out, and an explicit hand-off when your in-house team is ready to own it.

FAQ

Common questions.

If yours isn't here, write to us — we usually reply the same day.

What size of engagement do you take on?

Most engagements run between 6 weeks and 9 months. We've done short architecture reviews and we've embedded for over a year — but the sweet spot is a focused 2–4 month build with one or two senior engineers and a tech lead.

Do you work fixed-fee or time & materials?

Both. Discovery is always fixed-fee. After that, the build phase is usually time & materials with a hard ceiling and weekly burn reports. For well-defined scopes, we'll quote fixed-fee.

Where is the team based?

Silicon Valley, with engineers distributed across the US, UK, and EU. We work in your team's primary timezone and cover US Pacific, Eastern, and European hours by default.

Do you sign NDAs and assignment-of-IP?

Yes — standard practice on every engagement. Your code, your IP, full stop.

Can you work alongside our existing engineering team?

Absolutely — that's most of what we do. We code-review, pair, and follow your conventions. We're additive, not adversarial.

What does an engagement cost?

Discovery is typically $5–10K. Build engagements range from $25K (a focused 4-week sprint) to $250K+ (a multi-quarter platform build). We'll give you a clean number after the discovery week.

Have a hard problem?

We like hard problems.
